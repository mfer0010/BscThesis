This document contains some abbreviations commonly used in papers 
to do with CERN and the LHC and their meaning.

BCT - Beam Current Transformers
BLM - Beam Loss Monitor
BPM - Beam Position Monitor
BQM - Beam Quality Monitor
IQC - Injection Quality Check
LHC - Large Hadron Collider
MKI - LHC injection kicker system (LHC kicker Magnet)
RF  - Radiofrequency 
SIS - ??
SPS - Super Proton Synchrotron (Particle Accelerator)
TCDI- Transfer Line Collimators
